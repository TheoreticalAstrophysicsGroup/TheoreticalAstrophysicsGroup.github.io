About the Uchu Forum

The Tsukuba Uchu-Forum are a series of talks, which universities and research institutes in the Tsukuba area participate in. It is an event unique to the Kenkyugakuen-toshi.

Usual place and time:
  • Every second Wednesday of the month
  • 13:30 ~ 15:00
  • Center for Computational Sciences, University of Tsukuba, in the Workshop Room on the first floor

Usual place and time:
  • Every second Wednesday of the month
  • 13:30 ~ 15:00
  • Center for Computational Sciences, University of Tsukuba, in the Workshop Room on the first floor
