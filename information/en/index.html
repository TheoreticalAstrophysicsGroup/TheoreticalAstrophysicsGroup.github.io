Currently Held Sessions

Name Contributors Time About
1 Journal Club (Detailed Report) M1 and above Every Wednesday, 12:00 ~ 12:30 Presentation of a recent arXiv preprint paper
2 Journal Club (Flash Report) M1 and above, Volunteers Every Wednesday, 12:30 ~ 13:00 Concise summary of a recent arXiv preprint paper
3 Group Colloquia M1 and above, external visitors Every Wednesday, 13:30 ~ 15:00 Presentation / progress report of research achievements
4 Astrophysics seminar 4th year students Every Wednesday, 15:30 ~ 17:00 Group study using the text book "宇宙物理学" (Satou Fumitaka, Hara Tetsuya; Asakura Publishing)
5 Galactic Dynamics seminar M1, M2 Present sections from Galactic Dynamics (Binney & Tremaine, Princeton) and related research topics
6 Cosmology seminar M1, M2 Study sessions using the text book "現代宇宙論" (Matsubara Takahiko, Tokyo University Publishing)
7 Office Hour seminars Postgraduate and 4th year students Every Friday, 14:00 ~ 17:00 Supervision and discussion of research plan
8 Crossover seminars Postdocs and above, external visitors Second Thursday of the month, 13:40 ~ 17:00 Review lectures on current major topics or problems in Astrophysics
