About the Seminars

The Tsukuba Theoretical Astrophysics Seminars are colloquia that are given by invited or visiting speakers from outside our research group. If you would like to give a talk, please contact Masao Mori.

39th Seminar

Takuya Hashimoto

Osaka Sangyo University / NAOJ
