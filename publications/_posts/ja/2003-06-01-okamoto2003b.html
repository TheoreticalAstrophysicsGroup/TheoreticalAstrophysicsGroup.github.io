--- lang: ja abstract: We investigate environmental effects on morphology evolution of bright cluster galaxies (L > L[*]) in a Lambda -dominated cold dark matter universe using a combination of N-body simulations and semi-analytic (SA) model. We normalize our model parameters to reproduce the properties of local galaxies, and then we study how ram pressure stripping (RPS) and small starburst triggered by a minor merger (minor burst) affect the morphologies of cluster galaxies.
