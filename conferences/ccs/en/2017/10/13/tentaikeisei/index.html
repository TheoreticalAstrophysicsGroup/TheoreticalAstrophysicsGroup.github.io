Formation of Astrophysical Objects

Science and Motivation

This workshop aims to provide an opportunity to exchange views and share information about the current status of observations and theoretical research on a wide range of aspects concerning the formation of astronomical objects.

Moreover, it also serves as interim report by students in our research group who are working on dissertations.

Invited Talks

  • Ryoji Matsumoto (Chiba University)
  • Keijiro Miyazawa (Chiba University)
  • Tetsushi Kodama (Chiba University)
  • Ryoichi Nishi (Niigata University)
  • Shohei Minami (Niigata University)
  • Mariko Nomura (Keio University)
  • Shugo Michikoshi (Kyoto Women's University)

Important Dates

  1. Mon, Jul 31, 2017 : Registration Deadline
  2. Fri, Oct 13, 2017 : Workshop begins
  3. Sat, Oct 14, 2017 : Workshop ends
